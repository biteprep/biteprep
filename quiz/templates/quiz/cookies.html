Cookie Policy

Last Updated: August 3, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies to provide a functional experience on BitePrep.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for you to browse the website and use its features.
    • sessionid: A cookie from Django that keeps you logged in.
    • csrftoken: A cookie from Django used for security to prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks.
  • Third-Party Cookies:
    • Stripe: Our payment processor, Stripe, may set cookies that are necessary for their payment forms and services to function securely and correctly.

3. Use of Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage (which is similar to cookies but can store more information) to remember your preference for the light or dark theme. This is a preference setting and does not store personal information.

4. Your Choices

You can use your web browser settings to manage your cookie preferences. You can delete or block cookies, but be aware that if you do, some parts of our Service may not function correctly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at: biteprep@outlook.com.
